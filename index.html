Who am I ?

My name is Anıl Furkan Embel. I am 25 years old and I was born in Turkey. I completed my high school education at Kuleli Military High School. Between 2017 and 2021, I studied Industrial Engineering at the University of Turkish Aeronautical Association and graduated from my department. During my bachelor education, I participated in 2 Erasmus+ projects in Romania and Poland.

After the graduation, I managed Shopify, Amazon and Etsy e-commerce pages for 1 year in an American company and provided support to the company on issues such as remote data analysis and SEO. I realized that I have an interest in data analysis and my advanced communication skills and my interest in visual fields were effective in my decision to specialize in Data Analysis. I received an intensive 7-month boot camp training in Data Science. I did my internship in this field in a Belgian company in Brussels.

As for my hobbies, I was involved in athletics in high school and I love daily running. I also played American football as an amateur on the University team and I enjoy watching NFL games. I have a small YouTube channel with 9900 subscribers

In the future, I plan to produce projects in data science and business and further develop my skills in these areas.
